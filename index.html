Executive profile

I am a business builder with an engineer's understanding of how technology becomes a company.

My career began at 18, learning the technology industry from the ground up and developing real products for real clients. That early technical foundation became the operating advantage behind everything I built next.

While completing my master's degree at Glasgow Caledonian University, I worked in the UK as an Application and WordPress Engineer—combining postgraduate study with hands-on delivery in an international environment.

After graduating, I founded my own company, earned client trust, built commercial relationships, and expanded from delivery into leadership. Today, I lead Skuaberry and a multidisciplinary team delivering technology, AI and operating systems for international businesses.
